Units¶

For incompressible flow, IAMR supports any self-consistent units, as long as the length, time, and mass are consistent with the viscosity and diffusivity.

For low Mach number flow with temperature variations, the temperature solve requires a specific heat capacity, \(c_p\), which is currently hard-coded as \(1004.6 J kg^-1 K^-1\) (the specific heat capacity of dry air). Users can either keep this value, or change the specific heat capacity in their own version of the source code.
